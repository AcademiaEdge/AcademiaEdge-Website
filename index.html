Improve Your Coding Skills With AcademiaEdge

We are a non-profit organization aimed to teach students programming and problem-solving skills.

Register

Purpose

Through AcademiaEdge we hope to provide an alternative to expensive programming classes by offering completely free coding courses to the general public.

Created By

Sophomores at Carmel High School with advanced expertise in web development and coding, as well as a passion for education and learning.

Values

We promise to be responsible for the students we teach, giving them the highest quality education possible virutally. We value integrity, privacy, and honesty in our classes and workplace.

Structure

In order to give an individualized experience, each class has two teachers and is limited to 10-15 students. Classes are free and will fill up quickly so sign up soon!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the price for a class?

    Free! We are a non-profit organization, which means that our program is 100% free of charge.

  • What platform do you use for classes?

    Our classes are over Zoom and we will use Google Classroom to post class materials and assign homework.

  • Are you hiring volunteers to help?

    Yes, we are open to hiring students of all races from grades 7+ to help with our organization.

  • What do you teach?

    We here at AcademiaEdge are focused on providng as many courses as possible about coding.

  • How many classes do you have?

    We currently have 12 different classes, and we are hoping to expand the number in the following month.

  • Do we need to buy any textbooks for students?

    No, all the resources will be Google Classroom.

  • How long are your classes?

    Our classes range from 45 minutes to an hour long.

  • Is there homework for students?

    Yes, every week there will be homework posted. In the following week, we will go over homework in class.

  • How often are your classes?

    All courses follow the pattern of one class per week.

  • What if my question isn’t up here?

    Use the chat feature or send an email to us through the contact me option!
